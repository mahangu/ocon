NewsThe annual Duruthu Perahera of the Bolawalana Sri Jayasudharshanaramaya, Negombo will be held on January 10, birthday of late Minister Jeyaraj Fernandopulle. Bolawalana Sri Jayasudharshanaramaya was built using the personal wealth of Jeyaraj Fernandopulle. After his tragic death, his wife Dr. Sudarshini Fernandopulle was elected the head of the Dayaka Sabha.
