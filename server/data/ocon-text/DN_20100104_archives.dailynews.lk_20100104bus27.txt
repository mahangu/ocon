New MBA in HRM batch at PIM

A new batch of MBA students in HRM will commence its program of study and practice in January 2010. The program was inaugurated on December 18, 2009, at the PIM. PIM's MBA program blends theory and practice.

PIM believes that practice based on tested theories contributes towards high levels of performance. PIM's endeavour is to transform practitioners into professionals through its well structured 2-year program. Eagle Insurance Managing Director Deepal Sooriyaarachchi was the guest of honour at the inauguration.

PIM Director Dr Uditha Liyanage, and members of the faculty participated at the event.
