The new Member of Parliament M. Shafeek Rajabdeen was sworn in before Speaker W. J. M. Lokubandara at 9.30.a.m.in Parliament yesterday (8) to fill the vacancy created by assassination of Mr. T. Maheshwaran.

Rajabdeen who represents the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress had contested the last parliamentary election under the UNP list.

Before being nominated to Parliament Rajabdeen was an active member of the SLMC who was elected to the Colombo Municipal Council and later to the Western Provincial Council also as a SLMC member.
