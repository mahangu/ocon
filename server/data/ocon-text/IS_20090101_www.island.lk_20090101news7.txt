The Commissioner of Elections had filed an affidavit in the Supreme Court to state that Rs. 1,204,576.78 was incurred to recount the UPFA preferences polled North Central Provincial Council elections. This expense will have to be borne by the six petitioners who were the losing UPFA candidates and it will be in keeping with a Court order. The placing of candidates of the UPFA had not altered. However, a difference of 169 preferences had been recorded due to void ballots.

The petitioner are Udaya Nalin Wijeratne and five others. The first respondent is the Commissioner of Election.
