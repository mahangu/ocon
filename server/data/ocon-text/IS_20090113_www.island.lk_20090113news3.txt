Inspector General of Police Jayantha Wickremaratna has warned all Policemen involved in polls duty that irrespective of rank he would be compelled to punish those found inefficient or neglecting their duties.

Senior DIG (Elections) Gamini Navaratne said following instructions from President Mahinda Rajapaksa that public expectations of free and fair elections be ensured, the Inspector General had made it clear that he would not tolerate anyone shirking duties or acting in a partial manner towards any party or individual.

The IGP has conducted two seminars for Police officers from DIGs downwards in the two provinces and given strict instructions to on the implement of elections laws, removal of all cut outs, banners and posters displayed in violation of polls regulations.

Disciplinary inquiries have already been initiated against several Policemen, some of whom were senior officers for negligence and inefficiency.
