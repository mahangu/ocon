Online edition of Daily News - Lakehouse NewspapersJosephians Basketball Club Beat Dhamso Basketball Club by 54 points to 47 in the 'A' Division Girls Basketball Match played at St. Joseph's Girls School, Basketball Courts, Nugegoda on Saturday. Josephians BC led by 32 points to 22 at half time, Manarangi Jayasinghe and Imesha Thathsarani scored each 19 points for the winning team. Chethani Peiris scored 10 points.

Asa Chathurani was the top scorer for Dhamso BC. Kumudu Lakmali and Thivani Navodya contributed 10 points each for Dhamso Basketball Club.
