The Opposition members of Akmeemana Pradeshiya Sabha yesterday arrived for the sittings of the Council wearing black bands in protest against repression of the media. Opposition Leader Krishantha Pushpakumara, Bodhi Sandaruwan protested against the killing of 'Sunday Leader' editor Lasantha Wickrematunge and the burning of the Sirasa TV station.

The UPFA members Anil Priyadharshana and Sena Kehelwatte did not protest.
