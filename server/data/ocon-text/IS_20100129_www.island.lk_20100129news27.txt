KANDY - President of the Yatinuwara Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) branch Ms Wimala Mathurata and two others while returning home late afternoon on the Presidential election day (26) were attacked by a group of people. An entry to this effect had been lodged at the Kadugannawa police station.

Ms. Mathurata and the others were admitted to Kadugannawa hospital.

It was alleged that UNP member of the Kadugannawa Town Council and some of his accomplices were involved in the alleged attack.
