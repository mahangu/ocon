The Island-NewsDr. M. H. C. J. Ian has been appointed as an Ambassador General of the United Cultural Conventions of the USA to by the UCCA Biographical Institute.

Dr. Ian visited President Mahinda Rajapaksa and got his blessings. He was accompanied by Dr. Mrs. Sudarshani Fernandopulle, SLFP Chief Organiser for the Katana electorate and Ven. Ghanasiri Thero, the Incumbent of Angurukaramulla temple in Negombo.

He has also been selected for membership in the International Order of Ambassadors.
