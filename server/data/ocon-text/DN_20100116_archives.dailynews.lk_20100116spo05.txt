Sylvestrians on top

S. M. Jiffry ABDEEN Kandy Sports Correspondent

St. Sylvester's College, Kandy were on top at the end of the first day's play in their schools under 19 cricket tournament match against St. Aloysius College, Galle which began at police grounds, Kandy yesterday. The match will be continued today at 9.30 a.m.

Having dismissed the visiting team for 167 the Sylvestrians have put themselves in a strong position in scoring 99 for 1. The wrecker for St. Sylvester's was 15-year-old spinner Dushan Malalasena who took 5 for 52.
