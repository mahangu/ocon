NewsFormer UNP Cabinet Minister in President J.R. Jayewardene's government Dr. Wimal Wickremasinghe (66) died on Friday after a brief illness.

 Dr. Wickremasinghe held cabinet portfolios such as Minister of Environment, Planning and Policy and Parliamentary Affairs.

 He was also the Chief Government Whip at one time. Before entering politics, he was the Central Bank Director of Research.

 Dr. Wickremasinghe leaves behind his wife, retired Prof. Daya Wickremasinghe of the Faculty of Education, Kelaniya University and his two children Shresta (son) and Mauli (daughter), both residing in the USA.

 His funeral takes place on Monday 12 at 5 p.m. at the Kohuwala Public Cemetery.
