Claymore, explosives found

A claymore mine weighing 23 kilos and 2 kilograms of C4 explosives were recovered by police attached to the District Criminal Bureau on Monday evening inside a jungle patch in Kumankulam, Vavuniya.

Police spokesman SSP Ranjith Gunasekera said a police team attached to the DCB in Vavuniya deployed to apprehend a group of people illegally cutting trees in the Kumankulam jungle had found a claymore mine weighing 25 kgs and 2 kgs of C4 high explosives hidden in a thicket.
