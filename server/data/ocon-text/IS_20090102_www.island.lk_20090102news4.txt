The Island-NewsThe Police will conduct a census of people, on Sunday 04 2009 from 8.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m, who have moved from the Northern and Eastern Provinces and sought temporary or permanent residence in the Western Province after 2003,.

Police advises those who fall in to the above mentioned category to report to the closest centre where the sensus is conducted in the respective police division and register themselves,

Police also requests those who have got themselves earlier registered, to go to the same centre where they were registered.
