Enhancing personal security of MP State Counsel wants time to consult IGP

by Chitra Weerarathne

Senior State Counsel J. de Silva yesterday told the Court of Appeal that he needs time to get instructions from the IGP, Director, Ministerial Security Division and the DIG of the Ministerial Security Division, on the issue of enhancing the personal security granted to Mano Ganeshan, Colombo District MP and leader of the Western People's Front.

Ganeshan had complained that his security was unreasonably reduced, subsequent to December 18, 2007, when he voted against the government at the budget debate.

The Court granted time till January 30, 2008.

Ganeshan had asked for ten MSD security officers, one backup vehicle and a motor cycle.
