Inoka and Ajanthan road race winners

D. A. Inoka of Kundasale won the women's road race and Mahalingam Ajanthan of Gampola won the men's race conducted by the Ministry of Sports, Central Province held at the Peradeniya campus grounds on the January 10.

This race started and finished at the same venue. This race was considered as a selection trial, for the forthcoming road race in connection with the 21st national road race to be held at Ampara during the year.

Six runners were selected from the women's group, while 10 were selected from the men's group.
