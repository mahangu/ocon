Rapid agricultural development in A' pura

Rapid agricultural development is being achieved in the Anuradhapura District under the "Let's cultivate - Let's build the country" program successfully implemented in the area.

In addition to paddy cultivation farmers have been encouraged to take to maize, chillies and fruit-cultivation while facilities are being expanded to grow coconut and several other commercial crops.

North Central Chief Minister Berty Premalal Dissanayake explaining the record progress seen in the agricultural sector said that in 2008 maize cultivation covered 80,000 acres which produced 125,000 metric tons.

The farmers earned Rs 2,500 million out of the bumper harvest, he pointed out.
