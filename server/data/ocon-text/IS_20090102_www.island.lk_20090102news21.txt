The Island-NewsIn a mini cabinet reshuffle President Mahinda Rajapaksa took over the media ministry yesterday evening and put the incumbent Anura Priyadharshana Yapa in charge of the Investment Promotion Ministry.

Government sources said that it was a temporary move and there could be a few other changes as well.

It is learnt that Transport Minister Dullas Allahapperuma would take over as Media Minister shortly.

Amunugama, who was until yesterday Investment Promotion Minster, was appointed Public Administration Minister a position left vacant by Karu Jayasuriya's move back to the UNP.
