The Southern province crime prevention unit arrested a fisherman dynamiting fish. Four detonators and I Kg of Dynamite were taken into custody.

Southern province DIG Jayantha Gamage had received several complaints that dynamite was being used on a large scale for fishing in the Southern coastal areas. A Police team headed by ASP Damayantha Wijayasri has been assigned to stop the menace.

Galle Divisional Secretariat's officer in charge of explosives has been asked to submit a report on the issue of explosives.
