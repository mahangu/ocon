RABS junior tennis from 24

The inaugural Ratmalana Airport Building Society Junior Novices Tennis Tournament will be held from January 24 to February 15 at the new courts at Sirimal Uyana, Ratmalana. The tournament is sponsored by Dunlop Limited.

The matches are scheduled to start at 7.00 a.m. on January 24. The entries close on January 23 at 3.00 p.m.

There will be matches in singles in the under 10, 12, 14 boys and girls and doubles for under 14 boys and girls.
