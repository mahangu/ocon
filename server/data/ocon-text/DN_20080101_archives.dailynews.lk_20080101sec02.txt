Air Force attacks LTTE training base

The fighter jets of the Sri Lanka Air Force yesterday carried out an attack on LTTE training base complex in the North West of Oddusudan, Air Force's Spokesman Wing Commander Priyantha Weerasinghe said yesterday.

According to the Air Force's Spokesman the target was successfully hit by the Kfirs and MiG fighter jets around 7.15 to 7.45 yesterday morning.
