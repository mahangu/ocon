The All Ceylon Union of English Teachers (ACUET) unit of Education International has congratulated President Mahinda Rajapaksa on his re-election as Executive President of the Democratic, Socialist, Republic of Sri Lanka by a massive majority .

A special congratulatory message sent by Union Secretary-General M. A. Stanley Perera who is at the moment covering an educational tour in Western Australia says "Mahinda Rajapaksa is a leader with courage and vision. His ascendancy to power at this critical moment makes him a man of destiny for events that are taking place today and a turning point in our history. He possesses rare virtues fitting him for leadership".

The Union calls upon all teachers irrespective of party politics to extend their whole-hearted cooperation to him.
