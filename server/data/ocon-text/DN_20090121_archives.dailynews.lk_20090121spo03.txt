Chaminda, Ruwin help Fingara TC

Chaminda Vidanapathirana snapped up four wickets for 14 and Ruwin Peiris hammered an unbeaten half century to help Fingara Town Club beat Ceylinco SC by eight wickets in their MCA Premier league cricket tournament match worked off at Havelock Park yesterday.

Easy win for East West

A fine spell of bowling from T. M. Sampath (5 for 29) and an unbeaten half century from opener Sajith Fernando guided East West Marketing to a seven wicket victory over Seylan Bank in their MCA Premier league cricket tournament match worked off at Maitland Crescent yesterday.
