Lasting solution to climate change

Let us make a commitment today that we will cease to discuss the climate change problem in a theoretical manner at endless conferences and begin to adopt practical measures with a view to resolving it to the best of our abilities. It is my hope that we will be able to leave behind a livable land for our children and their

Dr. W. Sumathipala and C. Batuwitage two memebers of the Sri Lankan delegation at the Bella Centre

children,"

- Prime Minister Ratnasiri Wickramanayake

Sri Lankan Prime Minister Ratnasiri Wickramanayake's message was one among the prominently featured messages in the United Nations Framework Convention on the Climate Change (UNFCCC) Secretariat website under International calls for action.

The message called for the international leaders to find a lasting solution to climate change at the UN Climate summit.
