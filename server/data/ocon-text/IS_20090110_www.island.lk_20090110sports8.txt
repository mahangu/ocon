Light House Sports Club, Beruwela, recorded a thrilling win over Nawagampura Sports Club, Colombo, by one goal to nil in their All Island Inter Club Premier League 2008 Division II knock-out Soccer Tournament match played at Zahira College grounds in Dharga Town last Sunday.

The scores were nil all at lemons.

The tournament is organised and conducted by Football Federation of Sri Lanka.

Mohamed Nuzreth played an excellent game and scored the winning goal in the 58th minute. Light House SC will meet the winners of New Sun Rise SC Colombo/Civil Security SC at Dharga Town on Colombo.
