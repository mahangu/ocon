The 20th Annual General Meeting and get-together of Colombo branch of Anuradhapura Central College Past Pupils' Association, will be held on February 7 (Sunday) at 10.00 a.m. at the Auditorium of "Sakya", No. 8, Peters Place, Kohuwala, Nugegoda. The 20th Annual Souvenir will also be published on the occasion.

The Executive Committee of the Association wishes to invite all Old Pupils residing in Colombo and suburbs to attend the meeting.

For further details please contact: Hony. Secretary of the Association on 0777-150784.
