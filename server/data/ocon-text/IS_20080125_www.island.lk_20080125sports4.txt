Maris Stella College completed their first innings dominance over Thurstan College when they posted 238 runs in reply to Thurstan's 165, but the home team managed to better their first innings performance as they scored 136 for 2 at close, in their Inter School match which ended in a draw, at Thurstan Grounds on Wednesday.

St. Peter's College restricted De Mazenod College to 96 runs in reply to their 273 runs to record a first innings victory in their Inter School Under-13 Division I quarter-final played at Bambalapitiya on Wednesday.
