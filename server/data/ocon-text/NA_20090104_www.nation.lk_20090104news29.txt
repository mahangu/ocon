 With North Western and Central Provincial Council elections set to be held on February 14, all three main political parties are gearing up to win people's hearts and have organised major rallies with the participation of party stalwarts.



 According to the UPFA campaign schedule, President Mahinda Rajapaksa is to address seven major rallies, while Prime Minister Ratnasiri Wickremanayake is to preside over 33 election rallies. 



 The main opposition UNP said they have not finalised a campaign schedule yet. However, a party spokesman said that they are planning to kick-off their election campaign at a massive rally which is to take place in the Kurunegala town.



 The JVP will hold election rallies in Kandy, Kurunegala, Puttlam, Nuwara Eliya and Matale on January 5,7,9,12 and 15 respectively with the participation of their leaders.
