Hospital authorities yesterday made an urgent call for blood types 'O' Positive and 'O' Negative needed at the Moneragala Hospital to treat the injured in Buttala.

National Hospital Director Dr. Hector Weerasinghe said the patients who had sustained serious injuries would be rushed to Colombo in helicopters which left Colombo with a team of doctors to Monaragala.

The necessary medicine and surgical equipment which were not available in the Monaragala hospital were also despatched in the aircraft.
