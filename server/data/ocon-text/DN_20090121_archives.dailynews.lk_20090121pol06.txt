More crossover to UPFA

SANJAYA ASELA MATURATA

A group of United National Party activists from Yatinuwara electorate have joined the United People's Freedom Alliance and expressed their solidarity with the Government when they met President Mahinda Rajapaksa at President's House, Kandy last Saturday.

They included Kadugannawa Urban Councillor and former UC Chairman Mahinda Jayasinghe who has represented the Council for over 44 years, another senior councillor Nishantha Rupassara from Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha along with Councillors S.A. Gunatunge and A.L.M. Mahboob.

They were accompanied by Yatinuwara SLFP Chief Organiser Tikiri Kobbekaduwa when they met the President.
