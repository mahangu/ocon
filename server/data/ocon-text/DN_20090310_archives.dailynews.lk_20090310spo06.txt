Online edition of Daily News - Lakehouse NewspapersAn opening stand of 100 between Hiran Wickremasekara (66) and Dulash Kumarasinghe (41) for St. Anthony's was the highlight on the opening day of their inter-school match against Sri Sumangala College Panadura which began at Katugastota yesterday. Rain which fell at 3 p.m. washed out play for the day.

St. Anthony's 237 for 5 (Hiran Wickremasekera 66, Dulash Kumarasinghe 41, Malinda Perera 26, Adil Reyal 17, Kasun Ekanayake 29 not out, Jerome Siegertsz 37 not out, Buddhika Perera 4 for 46).
