A spokesman for the Health Ministry said yesterday that the production, packing and storing (PPS) plant constructed at a cost of 72 million rupees was inaugurated in Ratmalana by the Health Minister yesterday.

He said the machinery for manufacturing cost six hundred million rupees.

The SPMC hopes to manufacture approximately five hundred million tablets annually. The spokesman said the drugs worth 25 million rupees would be exported to countries like Fiji and -Papua New Guinea with assistance from the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA).

Quoting Health Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva the spokesman said that the Minister had urged the SPMC PPS to further increase production.
