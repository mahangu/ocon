Online edition of Daily News - Lakehouse NewspapersA ceremony including a salutation parade by the police, to mark the retirement of Senior Police Superintendant H. A. Ilangasiri, after 36-year service in the police department is scheduled to be held at 8.30 a.m. on February 7 at the Anuradhapura Public Stadium.

Many senior dignitaries in the armed services and public service are expected to participate on the occasion.
