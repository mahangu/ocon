Salaries of teachers to increase

Salaries of teachers will be increased under the guidance of the Ministry of Education and the court ruling based on materials submitted by the representatives of the Teachers' Unions in the case number SCFR 282/2008. These salary adjustments are to made in keeping with the circular 6/2006 which will be released by the Public Administration Ministry.

This particular circular has been finalised with the concurrence of both the National Salaries and Employees Commission and the Public Administration Ministry. It is now submitted to the Public Service Commission for approval.

Once it is approved, the Public Administration Ministry, in keeping with the earlier circular will issue a fresh one.

The Education Ministry further states that it always acts in accordance with court directives and necessary steps will be taken to rectify these salary anomalies no sooner they receive the approval of the Public Service Commission in this regard.
