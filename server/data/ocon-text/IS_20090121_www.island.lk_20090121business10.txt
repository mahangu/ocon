Prof. G.L.Peiris, Minister of Export Development and International Trade led a 23-member Business Delegation to the Dacca International Trade Exhibition in Bangladesh that is usually attended by frontline international businessmen, investors and industrialists.

Prof. Peiris and the Sri Lankan Delegation, consisting of several leading entrepreneurs, was received by Saffar Osman, Chairman of the Dacca Chamber of Commerce and Industry and Hussain Khalid, its former Chairman. Prof. Peiris also addressed the members of the Chamber on the trade and investment opportunities available in Sri Lanka.

Discussions also took place between members of the Delegation and those of the Dacca Chamber on matters relating to the strengthening of trade relations between the two countries.

In addition Prof. Peiris also met with Farook Khan, Minister of Commerce in the new Bangladeshi Government and discussed trade issues.
