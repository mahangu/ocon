A Judo Coaching Camp organised by Kano Judo School of Sri Lanka in collaboration with the Sri Lanka Judo Association was held from 2.00 p.m. to 5.00 p.m. yesterday at the Sugathadasa Sports Complex in Colombo.

Over 250 students from Colombo North Schools participated in the camp.

Basil Rodrigo (Sho Dan Black Belt), Chief Judo Instructor of Kano Judo School of Sri Lanka, rendered his support to make the camp a success along with the help granted by Kithsiri de Zoysa, President SLJA and KJSOSL.
