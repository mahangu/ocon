RATNAURA - Two hundred youth Rangers belonging to the 5th batch completed their training and 220 to 6th Batch were recruited at a ceremony in the Kegalle Beragala youth corps camp.

The Chief guest Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister of Sabaragamuwa said that due to the patriotic and valiant soldiers the areas forcibly occupied by the terrorists in the North have been liberated after three decades. In order to develop the liberated areas another war has to be waged. The youth corps as instructed by president Mahinda Rajapaksa, will have to take up such a task. They can accomplish any task if they have the determination and our youth would have got such determination and discipline during their training at the camp, he said.
