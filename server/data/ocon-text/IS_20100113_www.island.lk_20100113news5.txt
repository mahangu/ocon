Three ex-solders wounded in battle were assaulted by political goons while they were pasting posters of General Sarath Fonseka in Galpatha in the Kalutara district. They sustained head and leg injuries, the Media Unit of the common opposition candidate alleged on Monday.

The incident had occurred around 3am.

The victims were admitted to the Horana hospital. One of them who was in a serious condition was transferred to the National Hospital.
