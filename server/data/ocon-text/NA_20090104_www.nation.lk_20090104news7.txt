NewsSrilakvahini Satellite Television Channel has commenced its local transmission recently under the theme of 'New Sri Lanka to the world.' 

 This channel telecasts programmes 12 hours per day using Proto sat (98.5 East) satellite. In addition to telecasting news, current affairs programmes, documentary programmes, musical programmes, women and children programmes, teledramas and films. It also telecasts live programmes four days a week. Details of Srilakvahini Satellite Television Channel could be obtained by browsing www.srilakvahini.net website or by contacting telephone number 2821336.
