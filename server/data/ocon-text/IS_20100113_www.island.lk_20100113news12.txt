The Commissioner of Elections has given approval to the Returning Officers of all the districts to utilise private buses in addition to CTB buses to carry ballot boxes and for other connected election duties.

The Commissioner has approved Rs. 5,000 per day for buses with forty-two seats and Rs. 4,000 per buses with a seating capacity of 19-26.

Those buses would be utilised without a conductor. Rs. 350.00 per day would be paid as subsistence for dinner.
