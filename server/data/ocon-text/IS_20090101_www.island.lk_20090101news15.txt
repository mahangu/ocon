The government would lower bus fares by 4 per cent from next week as diesel price had been reduced, Media Minister Lakshman Yapa Abeyawardena said.

He told the media that Opposition Leader Ranil Wickremesinghe had stated that the Air Force did not have enough aircraft and helicopters to transfer security forces' casualties, as the aircraft were developing technical problems, but the SLAF had officially informed the government that his statement was baseless.

The Air Force had assured that injured security forces personnel would be airlifted by helicopters to the nearest hospitals and casualties of a critical nature taken to the National Hospital.

On the directives of President Mahinda Rajapaksa, an alternative budget had been introduced to sort out issues affecting transport, education, health, housing, Samurdhi, fertiliser subsidy, pension etc he said.
