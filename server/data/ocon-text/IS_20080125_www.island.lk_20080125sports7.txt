Sri Lanka Sonic Motor Sports (Pvt) Ltd., has introduced the ATV sport desert bike for leisure sport lovers for the first time in Sri Lanka.

The introduction was held recently, at Haritha Udyanaya Thalapathpitiya, under the patronage of Urban Development Authority Minister, Dinesh Gunawardana.

Chairman, Sonic Motor Sports (Pvt) Ltd., said that children aged between 5 to 10 years can use the 50cc and those who are above 10 years the 110cc, 150cc and 250cc modeld. "These bikes are available for leisure rides from 2.00 p.m. to 6.30 p.m. from Monday to Friday and 10.00 a.m. to 6.30 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday at Haritha Udyanaya, Thalapathpitiya. A fee will be charged for riding 5 laps which includes one free ride. Anyone who has not used such bikes could be trained in five minutes," he said.

Asoka said that in Hydarabad, freshers were trained in 20 minutes. This includes five minutes of briefing, five minutes of practice and ten minutes of riding and racing. He said that he was planning to introduce this system in Sri Lanka too.

Asoka had earlier introduced the Pro-Kart Outdoor Racing and Windsurfing in 2005 and 2006 at Gregory's Lake in Nuwara Eliya.
