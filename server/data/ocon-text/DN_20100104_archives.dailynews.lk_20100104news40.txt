Rs 2,700 b for health

The Government has spent Rs. 1,400 billion for the development of the health sector in 2009 while Rs. 2,700 billion has been allocated for the health sector this year, said Healthcare and Nutrition Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva.

The Health Minister was speaking at a ceremony organized by the Ministry staff.

The Minister said 2009 was a challenging year for the health sector.

"Many diseases like dengue, novel influenza, AH1N1 and leptospyrosis spread through many parts of the country. Over 33,000 dengue cases were reported while over 330 deaths due to dengue were reported last year.
