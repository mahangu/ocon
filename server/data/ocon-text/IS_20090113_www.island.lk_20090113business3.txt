Yesterdays trading resulted in turnover moving up from Rs.18million at the end of the previous day to a lacklustre Rs.45 million. The indices moved in opposite directions with the ASI gaining a marginal 1.54 points (0.10%) at 1533.79 while the MPI declined by 28.57points (-1.75%) at 1606.78.

ACLCables PLC informed the Colombo Stock Exchange that due to an unexpected decline in global Copper prices at the London Stock Exchange over the last few months, a significant loss has been experienced in its hedging contracts, and the overall quantification of the exposure would not be possible since it would depend over the fluctuations of copper prices over the next few months.
