The Island-NewsAn armed gang was put to flight by screaming security guards at the Kalahe People's Bank branch on Monday night, police said.

The gang had entered bank and shot at one of the two guards on duty before tying them up inside a toilet.

They had used explosives in an attempt to break open the rear of the bank but had failed.

But when the two guards began screaming out for help, the gang fled, police said.

The injured guard was admitted to the Karapitiya Teaching hospital. OIC Habaraduwa police Sujeewa Silva and Sub Inspector L. D. N. Karunaratne are investigating.
