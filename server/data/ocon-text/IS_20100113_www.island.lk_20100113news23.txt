Director of ITN teledrama, Pabaa, Shanika Soysa denied rumours that the main actress Upeksha Swarnamali has been taken out of the teledrama since she has appeared on the political stage of General Sarath Fonseka and canvassed on behalf of him.

Soysa said she would appear in episodes where she is due and dismissed rumours that she has been punished for appearing on General Fonseka's stage.
