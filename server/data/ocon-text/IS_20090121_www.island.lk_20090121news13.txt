Representative of the Government of Japan to Sri Lanka Yassushi Akashi will arrive in Colombo today (21). He will be here till January 25.

He has been specially dispatched by the Japanese Government in view of the recent developments in Sri Lanka and is scheduled to meet President Mahinda Rajapaksa and representatives of Opposition political parties to encourage them to work towards a political solution to the ethnic conflict, the Japanese Embassy announced yesterday. He is also scheduled to visit Trincomalee and Vavuniya to study the ground situation there.
