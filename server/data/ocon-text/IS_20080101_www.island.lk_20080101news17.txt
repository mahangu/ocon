The Sajini Weerasinghe Maternity Home gifted to Mawathagama state hospital by philanthropist and business tycoon Sasthri Rajaputhra Weerasinghe and Shirani Keppetipola Kumarihamy will be ceremonially opened by the First Lady Shiranthi Rajapakse on January 04.

The maternity home is equipped with modern facilities costing several millions of rupees.

Donor Sasthri Rajaputhra Weerasinghe residing in Japan has gifted the Sajini Weerasinghe Maternity Home to Mawathagama hospital as a token of goodwill to his birth place.

Most Venerable Udugama Sri Buddharakkitha Mahanayake Thero of Asgiriya and the Anunayake Theros of Malwatte and Asgiriya will be present at the ceremony.

Ministers Nimal Siripala de Silva, Jeyaraj Fernandopulle and S. B. Nawinna will also be present among others.
