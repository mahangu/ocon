Displaced Tamils, Muslims will be resettled - Minister

The displaced Tamil and Muslim people in the North will be resettled in their original locations when the mission against LTTE terrorists end, Plantation Industries Minister D.M. Jayaratne said.

"Most of these people are now living in Colombo and suburbs. The Government will soon create an environment in which they can live with dignity, the Minister said. Minister Jayaratne was speaking at the laying of a foundation stone for a three storied building complex at Illawathura Rahumaniya Muslim College, Gampola.

"The SLFP led government's have always stood by the minority communities when they are in power and ensured their privileges and rights. The Prophet's Birthday was also declared a national holiday under the United Left Front Government," he said.
