The Government has set aside a sum of Rs. 3 million to pay compensation to the next of kin of those who were killed in yesterday's claymore mine blast at Helagama, Buttala.

As an immediate relief measure, Rs 25,000 will be paid to the next of kin of an unmarried person killed and Rs 50,000 to the next of kin of a married person. In addition, a sum of Rs 15,000 would be paid to the family of those killed to meet the funeral expenses. An injured person will be paid Rs 10,000 initially. The Moneragala District Secretariat has been asked to effect the initial payments immediately and to pay the balance after receiving the death certificates.

The Government pays a maximum of Rs 100,000 for a death and Rs 50,000 to the injured.
