No major incidents - PAFFREL

Nadira Gunatilleke

The People's Action for Free and Fair Election (PAFFREL) records that there were no major incidents reported from any part of the country up to 11 am yesterday and voting progressed smoothly. Voter turn out was high, Executive Director,PAFFREL, Rohana Hettiarachchi said.

According to Hettiarachchi, so far only 15 minor incidents reported from some parts of the country.

Among the incidents reported was a case of shooting from Katupotha, Galle where someone shot in the direction of a house.

There were a few incidents of chasing away and threatening polling agents. Voting progress smoothly in Jaffna despite one minor incident reported from No.28 polling station.
