Couple nabbed for misusing credit card

Chaminda Perera

A couple was nabbed by Mirihana police for attempting to purchase jewellery from a shop in Nugegoda town by using a stolen credit card. According to Mirihana police, the couple had failed to provide details about the card when inquired by the cashier. The cashier had phoned the bank from which the card was issued and found that he was not the real owner.
