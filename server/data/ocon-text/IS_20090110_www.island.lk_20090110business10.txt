Coca-Cola Beverages Sri Lanka Limited was the Silver Sponsor of the Vedasa Medical Exhibition 2009, organized by the Faculty of Medical Sciences of University of Sri Jayewardenepura.

The biggest public medical event of the year, the exhibition is being held to celebrate the Golden Jubilee of the University from the 06th-12th January 2009 at the BMICH. Coca-Cola Sri Lanka is proud to be associated with one of the key providers of higher education to the nation, and salutes the University of Sri Jayewardenepura for 50 years of service!
