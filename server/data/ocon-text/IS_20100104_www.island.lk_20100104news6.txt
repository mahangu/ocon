The Health Ministry will provide an emergency unit for the Ratnapura Teaching Hospital at a cost of Rs. 450 million. Ministry spokesman W. M. D. Wanninayake told The Island that this was the fourth unit set up. Waninayake said that work on the Ratnapura hospital unit would commence today (January 4).

According to him, emergency units were established at Kurunagala, Kandy and Karapitiya hospitals at the cost of Rs. 450 million each.
