No major incidents - IGP

No major elections violence incidents reported during the polls yesterday, said Inspector General of Police Mahinda Balasuriya.

Earlier the IGP gave special instructions to all OICs of the Police stations to take stern action against any person or persons, irrespective of their position who attempt to violate election laws.

IGP Balasuriya appealed to the public and the supporters of political parties to keep away from public places to avoid any incidents. Be at home until the election results are announced, the IGP added.
