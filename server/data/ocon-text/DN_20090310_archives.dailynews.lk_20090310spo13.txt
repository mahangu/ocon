Online edition of Daily News - Lakehouse NewspapersDehiwala YMCA beat Ambalangoda Basketball Club by 86 points to 68 in the 'C' Division Basketball Semi-final match played at St. Joseph's Girls School Basketball Courts, Nugegoda recently.

Dehiwala YMCA led by 33 points to 18 at half time. Rashan Ruwanga and Diluic Fernando scored 25 points each for the winning team. S. Madusanka was the top scorer for the Ambalangoda BC with 25 points, N. Hasaranga collected 14 points. Josephians Basketball Club beat CSE by 83 points to 69 points in the 'C' Division Semi final played at same courts.
