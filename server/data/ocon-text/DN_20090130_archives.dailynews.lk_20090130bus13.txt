Prof. Pieris leads delegation

Minister of Export Development and International Trade Prof. G.L. Peiris led a 23-member business delegation to Dhaka International Trade Exhibition in Bangladesh which was attended by frontline international businessmen, investors and industrialists.

The delegation was received by Chairman of the Dacca Chamber of Commerce and Industry Saffar Osman and its former Chairman Hussain Khalid and Prof. Peiris also addressed the members of the Chamber on the trade and investment opportunities available in Sri Lanka.
