The Island-NewsSouthern Province Chief Minister Shan Vijayalal de Silva last week ordered that the Southern Province Education Secretary submit a report on the activities of a certain organisation which was using school buildings in the province claiming that it was conducting classes for the students after school hours. The Chief Minister's order followed a complaint made by JVP member of the Council Nalin Hewage.

"The organisation had been charging the students for question papers and had

obtained over Rs 16,000 each from the youth promising that they would be recruited as teachers," reliable sources said.
