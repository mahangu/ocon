A toddler who was walking between his mother and a relative, both holding his hands, received fatal injuries on (24) when a drunken motor cyclist rode between them and knocked down the toddler.

The speeding cyclists hit a bush, over turned but managed to escape while the mother Kumudu Abeygunawardena (30) an employee of Aluthkade Courts Complex and the other woman were frantically engaged in finding a vehicle to rush the injured boy into the hospital.
