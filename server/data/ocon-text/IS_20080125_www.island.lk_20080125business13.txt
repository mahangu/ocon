Ministers of Foreign Affairs and Export Development and International Trade, Ambassadors and High Commissioners of all the SAARC countries and a cross- section of Sri Lankan businessmen are expected to gather at Cinnamon Grand Hotel on February 5 for the official launch of the SAARC Trade Fair that will be held in Sri Lanka this year.

The highlight of the day would be the official launch of SAARC Trade Fair, unveiling of the Logo of SAARC Trade Fair and the inauguration of the Official Web site of SAARC Trade Fair.

All the details of this important Trade Fair will also be revealed on that day.

The venue will also provide opportunity for social integration of top level businessmen besides allowing them to meet and discuss trade opportunities and related information with SAARC Ambassadors and High Commissioners.
