Biyagama PS Budget for 2010 passed

Mahanama Vithanage - Malvana Group Corr.

The Year 2010 Budget of the Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha was unanimously passed last week.

It was presented by Chairman M.A.M. Prasanna Sampath (UNP). According to estimates the General Revenue was Rs 114,005,300 including capital grants and recurring and capital expenditure was Rs 113,961,180 surplus was Rs 44,120. UNP and PA members voted for the Budget, while the JVP members left the Chamber.
