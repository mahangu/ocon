The National Housing Development Authority (NHDA) has announced that it had launched a programme to build 1,000 houses expeditiously for needy families in the Northern Province.

The programme is intended cater to families displaced by the war in the Jaffna, Vavuniya, Mannar, Mullaitivu and Kilinochchi districts. The NHDA will meet the cost for building 500 houses and financial assistance for the balance 500 houses will be given from the funds of proposed housing development programme planned by the government for the North.

The housing development programmes covering all the districts of Northern Province will be implemented in 03 stages and the first stage of this programme will be implemented from January to April 2009.
