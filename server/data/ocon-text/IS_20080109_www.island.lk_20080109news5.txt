Minister of Foreign Employment Promotion and Welfare Keheliya Rambukwella on Monday expressed hope that the MoU to be signed between Sri Lanka and Qatar today (9) would go a long way in strengthening the Bi-lateral relations between the two countries.

He said it would help address the security concerns of Sri Lankan migrant workers and open doors to increased employment opportunities for skilled workers with added benefits and welfare facilities.

On Wednesday January 9 the Minister of Labour and Social Services of Qatar Dr. Sultan Bin Hassan Al Dhabit al Dousari and the Minister of Foreign Employment Promotion and Welfare Keheliya Rambukwella are scheduled to sign an MoU at the Ministry auditorium in Colombo at the auspicious time of 10.44 a.m., Information Secretary to the Ministry Lalith Ravin Bopitiya said .

Secretary to the Ministry of Foreign Employment Promotion and Welfare R. V. Nanayakkara and the Chairman of the Sri Lanka Foreign Employment Bureau (SLFEB) Kingsley Ranawaka will be present.
