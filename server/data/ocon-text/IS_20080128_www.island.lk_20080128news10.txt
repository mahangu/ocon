Province increased with the killing by suspected LTTE gunmen of two TMVP cadres in Ampara as the government promised security to all the candidates contesting the Local Government elections in the Batticaloa District. Another TMVP cadres who had been abducted on Friday was released yesterday and admitted to hospital.

DIG Eastern Range V. S. A. P. Nanayakkara went on record saying that the government was ready to provide protection to all candidates.

There are 831 candidates from registered political parties and independent groups in the fray for 101 seats. Elections will be conducted at 285 polling booth and 270,471 people are eligible for voting.
