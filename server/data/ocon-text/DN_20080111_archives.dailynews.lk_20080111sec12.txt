Did Prabhakaran's wife visit TN ?

Speculation was rife in Rameswaram whether the wife of LTTE leader V Prabhakaran arrived there on Wednesday along with a group of Sri Lankan refugees.

Police and media personnel rushed to the Mandapam refugee camp after rumours said Prabhakaran's wife Madhivadhani reached the camp early yesterday morning. There were also rumours that Madhivadhani met the refugees.
