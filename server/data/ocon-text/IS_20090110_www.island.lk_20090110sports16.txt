Two unbeaten tons from Jeevantha Kulatunga and middle-order bat Roshane Silva helped Colts CC stage a fine recovery from early hiccups to reach a solid total of 270 for four in their first innings on the opening day of their SLC Premier League Tier 'A' tournament match played against Ragama CC at Havelock Park, Colombo, on Friday (9).

Kulatunga scored 130 from 115 deliveries inclusive of three sixes and 18 fours while Silva remained unbeaten on 109 with a six and 16 boundaries. The pair has put on 238 runs for the fifth wicket so far after their team was struggling at 32 for four at one stage.
