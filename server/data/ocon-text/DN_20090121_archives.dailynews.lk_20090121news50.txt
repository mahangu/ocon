Maha paddy purchase tops 140,000 mt

The Paddy Marketing Board (PMB) has purchased 140,000 metric tons of paddy during the Maha season of 2008/2009.

PMB General Manager Vajira Perera told the Daily News the 140,000 metric tons of paddy will be stored as buffer stock in Government owned stores.

The Board is engaged in the paddy purchasing program to issue sufficient paddy stocks to the market if there is an emergency, Perera said.

The Board expects to purchase paddy covering 30,000 mt in the Eastern Province, 50,000 mt in Polonnaruwa, 30,000 mt in Anuradhapura, 15,000 mt in Wayamba and 15,000 mt in the South. It will continue in other districts as well.
