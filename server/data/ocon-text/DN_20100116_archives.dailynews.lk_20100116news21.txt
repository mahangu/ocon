CPA Secy- Generalhere today

Dr. Shija

Commonwealth Parliamentary Association (CPA) Secretary General Dr. William Ferdinand Shija, will be arriving in Sri Lanka today. He will be here until January 20. The main objective of the visit is to conduct preliminary discussions with Speaker W. J. M. Lokubandara, Secretary-General of Parliament Dhammika Kitulgoda and other officials regarding the 58th CPA conference to be held in Sri Lanka.

A Tanzania, Dr. Shija holds a doctorate in Mass Communication from Howard University, USA and has been a member of the National Assembly of Tanzania from 1990 to 2005.

He has held various ministerial posts during his political career and has authored numerous academic and parliamentary papers in communication and development.

Dr. Shija assumed duties as CPA Secretary-General on January 1, 2007.

Dr. Shija will meet with several dignitaries including the Speaker. He is also scheduled to travel to Kandy to visit the Sri Dalada Maligawa, the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens and the Elephant Orphanage at Pinnawala.
