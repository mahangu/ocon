The Rugby season for the year 2008 will kick off in February with the Inter Club Rugby 7's tournament, Sri Lanka Rugby Football Union announced yesterday.

After the Rugby Asiad in Colombo, where Sri Lanka finished runners-up, the players have had a long lay off and the national players will be in action from 2nd of February.

This particular Rugby 7's tournament is organized in three different phases in Kandy, Colombo while the other venue is yet to be confirmed. The tournament will get underway in Kandy on 2nd of February and will shift to Colombo for the finals.

This particular tourney will also serve as the platform for the selectors to pick the best and in form players to represent the country in the forthcoming Hong Kong International Rugby 7's tournament.
