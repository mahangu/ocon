The chief candidate of the Illankan-Tamil Areseckachchi of Batticaloa V. Indrakumar has complained to the Supreme Court that a free and fair local government poll could not be held in the Eastern Province.

The petitioner had said that the Pillayan group of the Tamil Viduthan Party had resorted to violence and the intimidation of person in the Eastern Province.

The right to franchise of petitioner and the other electors will be breached if a poll is held under such a situation.

The petition requested the Court to void the gazette notification that called for nominations to hold a local government poll in this province.

Among the respondents are the Elections Commissioner and the Attorney General.
