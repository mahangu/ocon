British Deputy High Commissioner Mark Gooding yesterday (12) undertook a two-day field visit to Jaffna for a first hand assessment of the humanitarian and the security situations prevailing in the area.

He was due to meet the newly appointed Security Forces Commander of Jaffna, the Bishop of Jaffna and the Heads of INGOs. In addition, he was due to visit an IDP camp to get a first hand view of the conditions faced by the IDPs. He was to also to visitthe Nallur Hindu Kovil and the historic Jaffna Library.
